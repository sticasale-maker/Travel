How to read this. The map and charts are a relative pattern, not a probability — iNaturalist is presence-only, so a beach with more dots may just have more people with phones. The forecast model in the app corrects for this with a matched case–control design; this explorer is the raw picture behind it. Two real signals survive that caveat: facing direction (most beaches open ESE 100–120°, but Malabar, Cabbage Tree Bay and Shelly face NE and peak in a different season) and the north–east seasonal split (northern beaches peak in spring, the eastern ones in autumn). 84 records carried only a fuzzy “Sydney” location and aren’t mapped.
